How I treat projects

I use projects as a way to learn by building. Some become polished pieces of work, some remain experiments, and some simply teach me enough to move on to the next idea.

The important part is that each one leaves something behind: a better understanding of architecture, data, interfaces, infrastructure, debugging, or how to keep improving a system over time.
