About Nate&Co

A personal website, digital workshop, and place to build things.

Nate&Co is my personal corner of the web — part portfolio, part project archive, and part testing ground for ideas I want to explore properly.

Regular, Medium, SemiBold, Bold, and Italic.

What this site is

This site started as an experiment and proof of concept, but it is gradually becoming a more structured home for my projects, learning, and technical ideas.

It brings together web experiments, small tools, project pages, and work-in-progress ideas under one simple static website.

What I build

I enjoy building practical software that solves real problems, especially around automation, data handling, web development, and small self-hosted systems.

Some projects are serious, some are playful, but most exist because I wanted to understand how something works by building it myself.

The longer-term idea

The goal is for this site to become a digital workshop: somewhere I can document what I am learning, showcase finished projects, host small experiments, and keep track of the systems I build over time.

Rather than being a static portfolio that rarely changes, I want it to grow alongside my skills and reflect the journey from small experiments into more complete, well-structured projects.
